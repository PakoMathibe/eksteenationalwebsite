Who We Are

Eksteen National is a leading Insurance Claims Solutions company with over 20 years of experience in the insurance sector. Headquartered in Pretoria, South Africa, we specialize in providing comprehensive claims services to individuals and businesses across the Gauteng Region. With regional representation in Johannesburg and Cape Town, we extend our expertise and support to a wider clientele base.

Thys Eksteen (MD & Owner)

23 Years Experience

Thys M Eksteen is the national managing director & owner. He has 23 years of experience as an adjuster in the non-motor personal and commercial claims space. He has completed his Short Term Insurance Associateship studies through UNISA and achieved this in 2015.

More Than 15 000 Completed Assessments

Having started his career as a Loss Adjuster in 1997, he has been operating in this space on behalf of Insurers and their Intermediaries and has completed more than 15 000 assessments, including Personal Lines, Engineering, Large Fire and Commercial claims.
