Who We Are as Eksteen National

Eksteen National is a leading Insurance Claims Solutions company with over 20 years of experience in the insurance sector. Headquartered in Pretoria, South Africa, we specialize in providing comprehensive claims services to individuals and businesses across the Gauteng Region. With representation in Gauteng, we extend our expertise and support to a wider clientele base.

Our mission is to provide exceptional insurance claims solutions, guided by expertise, innovation, and a customer-centric approach.

Our vision is to be the leading and most trusted insurance claims solutions company in the industry.

We uphold the highest standards of honesty, transparency, and ethics in all our interactions. Trust is at the core of our relationships with clients, partners, and employees.

Thys Eksteen - Managing Director and Owner
